Taxonomies are a way to describe and categorize products and projects, under some criteria. And they take the format of a URN, like:

~~~bash
urn:example:text
~~~

They are used extensively in the definitions of:

- [areas](./areas.md);
- [categories](./areas.md);
- [statuses](./status.md).
